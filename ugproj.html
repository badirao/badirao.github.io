UG Research Project

For our Final Year Project, We developed a Home Assistant, akin to google home, that was capable of understanding and responding to code mixed Hindi-English and Kannada-English Speech. Eg. Nange mooru Chips packet beku, mujhe ek train chaiye Delhi to Bangalore ko etc.

We won the best project award for the same, and its currently deployed in telegram. A paper is underway.
